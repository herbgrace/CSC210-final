My Future Plans

I am not a product of my circumstances. I am a product of my decisions.

—Stephen Covey

This quote resonates with me because it reminds me that anything can be changed if I try hard enough. Using my current situation as an excuse is exactly that, an excuse. I need to have the willpower and dedication to make change if I want anything to happen.

What comes after graduation?

After graduating from Neumont, I want to work as a software engineer. This career should supply me with everything I need to live comfortably and happily. I want to continue with my hobbies related to computers/electronics and mechanics. I would like to learn more about designing computer parts, and possibly make my own computers for fun. The way I see it, a job is not the final goal, it's merely a stepping stone in the path to fulfillment.
